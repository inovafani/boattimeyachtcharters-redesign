Our yachts are berthed at Muriel Henchman Public Pontoon, Gold Coast. Plentiful free parking is available at Marina Mirage/Mariners Cove — approximately 10 minutes from Surfers Paradise and 15 minutes from Broadbeach.

We primarily operate in the calm, protected waters of the Gold Coast Broadwater. For offshore whale watching cruises there is some ocean swell, though most guests find conditions very comfortable aboard our large, stable superyachts.

We can arrange DJ or live band entertainment, professional photography and videography, watersports packages including banana boats and SUPs, and a full range of catering upgrades to elevate your experience.

Fill out our booking enquiry form or book cruise tickets directly online. Our team will quickly get back to you with availability, pricing, and inclusions. A deposit secures your preferred date — we recommend booking early as dates fill fast.
